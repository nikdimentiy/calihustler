METRICS GUIDE
How to read every number on your dashboard
🔥 North Star — check these first every day
Your primary signals. If these look good, the system is working.
Hustle Score
lifetime · cumulative
Your all-time productivity score — every completed mission adds points, bonus points for perfect days. It only ever goes up.
→ This is the single number that proves long-term consistency. Watch it grow week over week.
Current Streak
daily · min 3 missions to count
Consecutive days where you completed at least 3 missions. Days with fewer than 3 break the chain — partial effort doesn't sustain the streak.
→ Growing = your system is holding. Drops to zero = either a missed day or a day with under 3 missions — get back above the threshold.
Today Completed
live · resets at midnight
Missions finished today out of the total. Instant snapshot of how your current day is going.
→ Quick gut-check. If it's late in the day and the number is low, you still have time to push.
📈 Trend — direction of movement
Smoothed signals that filter out single-day noise. Use these to spot real momentum shifts.
7-Day Avg
rolling · last 7 days
Average missions completed per day over the past week. Smooths out outlier days — one bad day won't tank it, one great day won't inflate it.
→ High avg = healthy, sustainable system. Dropping avg = something is slipping before your streak even breaks.
This Week
current week · Mon–today
Total missions completed since the start of this week. Useful for weekly planning and comparing week-to-week pace.
→ Easy to benchmark: if last week was 42, are you on pace to match or beat it by Sunday?
Month vs Last
month-over-month
Current month's total missions compared to the previous month. Shows whether you're improving or regressing on a longer timeframe.
→ + number = growth. − number = a dip worth investigating. Small swings are normal; large dips need a fix.
📊 Context — historical benchmarks
Reference points that give your daily numbers meaning. More data = more accurate picture.
Best Streak
all-time · personal record
Your longest consecutive-day run ever. Proves the system is capable of working at its peak — you've done it before.
→ If Current Streak is close to Best Streak, you're operating near your historical ceiling. Push past it.
Total Days Logged
all-time · cumulative
Total number of days you've recorded activity. The larger this number, the more reliable every other metric on the dashboard becomes.
→ Under 14 days: trends are rough estimates. Over 30: patterns are real. Over 90: your data is trustworthy.
Perfect Days
all-time · 100% days
Days where you completed every single mission. Each one proves you're fully capable of hitting the ceiling.
→ Even rare perfect days matter — they show the ceiling exists and is reachable. Aim to increase the frequency.
🔍 Diagnostic — where to optimise
Pinpoints specific weaknesses in your system so you can fix the right thing.
Weakest Day
by day of week · avg
The day of the week with the lowest average mission count across all weeks. Reveals a structural gap in your schedule — not bad luck, a pattern.
→ If it's Monday: your week starts slow — fix the morning routine. If it's Friday: you're losing focus before the weekend.
Top Mission
all-time · most completions
The mission you've completed most often across all recorded days. This one is already running on autopilot — it's become a real habit.
→ This is proof habits can be built. Now apply the same consistency to your lowest-completion missions.
Since Perfect Day
days elapsed · last 100%
Days elapsed since your last 100% mission day. Keeps the peak visible so it doesn't fade from memory.
→ Under 7: you're sharp. 7–14: due for another push. Over 14: the system may be too complex — simplify or recommit.
PRODUCTIVITY USER GUIDE
Master the system · Dominate every day
🚀 Getting Started
  • 01 Set your wake time — use the wake time input at the top to log when you started your day. This anchors your Prime Day Launch mission and calculates your daily time blocks.
  • 02 Pick your energy level — select LOW, MEDIUM, or HIGH each morning. Your energy level is stored daily and influences how you interpret your mission completion rate.
  • 03 Complete your 10 daily missions — check off each mission as you finish it. Missions are tracked per day. Completing at least 3 missions keeps your streak alive.
  • 04 Review your metrics panel — after completing missions, scroll up to the metrics panel to track your Hustle Score, streak, and weekly averages in real time.
  • 05 Review past days — click any date on the calendar to review what missions were completed. Use this to spot patterns in your consistency.
⚡ Daily Power Routine
Win the morning, own the day
Complete Prime Day Launch (Mission 10) within the first 90 minutes of waking. It sets the tone for every mission that follows. A strong morning creates momentum that compounds through the day.
Stack your missions by energy
On HIGH energy days — tackle Learn Core, Intellectual Feast, and Conclusion Journal in the morning while your mind is sharpest. Save physical missions for midday. On LOW energy days — lead with Energy Recovery and Sculpted Physique to recharge the system.
Use the Mission Weight Matrix (press X)
Not all missions are equal. Weight-3 missions (highest impact) should be prioritized when time is short. Open the Weight Matrix to see today's score and which missions give you the most points.
Log before midnight
The system resets at midnight. Always check your missions before bed — even 5 minutes of journaling or a quick finance review counts. Partial wins are better than blank days.

🔥 Building & Protecting Your Streak
The 3-mission minimum rule
Your streak only counts on days with 3 or more completed missions. On busy or low-energy days, identify your 3 highest-weight missions and complete at least those. Protect the chain above all else.
Never miss twice
Missing one day breaks the streak but is recoverable. Missing two days in a row starts a downward spiral. The rule: never miss twice. One off day is rest. Two is a new bad habit forming.
Use your Best Streak as a target
When your Current Streak approaches your Best Streak, you're operating at your personal peak. Open the Metrics Guide (press G) to see the full breakdown. Each time you break your record, the ceiling rises.
💡 Pro Tips & Power Features
Keyboard shortcuts are your edge
Keys 1–0 toggle missions instantly. K shows shortcuts, G opens the Metrics Guide, X opens Weight Matrix. Use them to log missions in seconds without scrolling.
Check your 7-Day Average, not just the streak
The 7-day average catches slow decline before your streak breaks. If the average is dropping, your system is slipping — adjust before it hits zero. A rising average means sustainable momentum.
Use the calendar to spot your patterns
Look at the calendar heat map weekly. Which days of the week are consistently weak? Are you strong on Monday but dead by Friday? Adjust your routines to front-load the week's hardest missions.
